## Purpose

Ready-to-use citation format templates for the most common source types. Used by the citation extraction skill as a quick reference when formatting citations.

## APA 7th Edition

### Journal Article
```
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. Title of Periodical, volume(issue), page-page. https://doi.org/xxxxx
```

### Book
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle. Publisher. https://doi.org/xxxxx
```

### Edited Book Chapter
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In E. E. Editor & F. F. Editor (Eds.), Title of book (pp. xx-xx). Publisher. https://doi.org/xxxxx
```

### Website
```
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Site Name. https://www.example.com/page
```

### Conference Paper
```
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Days). Title of paper [Paper presentation]. Conference Name, Location.
```

## Chicago (Notes-Bibliography)

### Journal Article (Footnote)
```
1. First Last, "Article Title," Journal Title volume, no. issue (Year): page.
```

### Journal Article (Bibliography)
```
Last, First. "Article Title." Journal Title volume, no. issue (Year): page-page. https://doi.org/xxxxx.
```

### Book (Footnote)
```
1. First Last, Title of Book (Place: Publisher, Year), page.
```

### Book (Bibliography)
```
Last, First. Title of Book. Place: Publisher, Year.
```

## Harvard

### Journal Article
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of article', Title of Journal, volume(issue), pp. page-page. doi:xxxxx.
```

### Book
```
Author, A.A. (Year) Title of book. Edition. Place: Publisher.
```

## IEEE

### Journal Article
```
[1] A. A. Author and B. B. Author, "Title of article," Title of Journal, vol. X, no. X, pp. xx-xx, Month Year.
```

### Conference Paper
```
[1] A. A. Author, "Title of paper," in Proc. Conference Name, Year, pp. xx-xx.
```
