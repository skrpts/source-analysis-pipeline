## Purpose

Reference document for APA 7th edition citation formatting. Used by the citation extraction skill and summarisation prompts.

## In-Text Citations

### Single Author
- Parenthetical: (Smith, 2023)
- Narrative: Smith (2023)

### Two Authors
- Parenthetical: (Smith & Jones, 2023)
- Narrative: Smith and Jones (2023)

### Three or More Authors
- Parenthetical: (Smith et al., 2023)
- Narrative: Smith et al. (2023)

### Direct Quotes
- Short (under 40 words): "quoted text" (Smith, 2023, p. 15)
- Long (40+ words): block quote, indented, no quotation marks

## Reference List Format

### Journal Article
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*, *volume*(issue), page-page. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Book
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle*. Publisher.

### Book Chapter
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In E. E. Editor (Ed.), *Title of book* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher.
