## Capability

Analyses source material to identify and structure the argumentative components: central claims, supporting evidence, assumptions, counter-arguments, and rebuttals. Produces a visual-ready argument map showing the logical relationships between components.

## When to Use

- Analysing the strength of an argument in an academic paper
- Comparing argumentative structures across multiple sources
- Preparing for critical analysis or response writing
- Identifying logical fallacies or unsupported claims

## Inputs

Source text, focus area (if analysing a specific argument within a larger text)

## Outputs

Structured argument map: central claim, premises, evidence for each premise, assumptions, counter-arguments, rebuttals, and an assessment of overall argument strength
